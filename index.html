About 5 Star

5 Star is a delicious candy bar made by Cadbury, one of the largest chocolate manufacturers in the world. This candy bar is a perfect blend of creamy chocolate, crunchy nougat, and smooth caramel. It has been one of my favorite candies since childhood.

Interesting Facts:

  • 5 Star was introduced in India in the year 1969
  • It was originally available in just one flavor, chocolate
  • It has won the "Most Trusted Brand" award in the chocolate category multiple times
  • In 2019, Cadbury launched a new variant of 5 Star called "5 Star 3D" which has a unique shape and texture

Nutrition Facts

A single 5 Star candy bar (45 g) contains:

  • Calories: 225
  • Total fat: 10.8 g
  • Cholesterol: 9 mg
  • Sodium: 92 mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 29.4 g
  • Protein: 2.7 g
